Privacy Policy for NordBot

At NordBot, accessible through Discord, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by NordBot and how we use it.

Information We Collect

NordBot may collect user data such as usernames, message content, and server interactions for the purpose of providing functionality and services within the Discord server. We do not share or sell this information to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide, operate, and maintain our bot functionalities within the server. This may include managing roles, sending messages, and interacting with server members.

Data Retention

We only retain user data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting the server administrator.

Your Data Protection Rights

As a user of NordBot, you have the right to request access to your data, correction of any inaccurate information, and deletion of your data. To make a request, contact the server administrator.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through Discord.
